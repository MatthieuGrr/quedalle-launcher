Quedalle is a minimal, tile-based Android home screen built with Jetpack Compose. It is designed for users who want full control over their home screen layout without any bloat, tracking, or accounts.

TILE TYPES

- App tiles: launch any installed app, with live notification badge counts
- Spacer tiles: colored blank tiles to structure your grid visually
- Divider tiles: thin horizontal lines to separate groups of tiles

CUSTOMIZATION

- Drag and drop to reorder any tile
- 12 color presets per tile, plus app-color and transparent options
- Inline app search to find and launch apps quickly

PRIVACY

- No internet permission
- No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, no ads
- Open source under GPL-3.0
