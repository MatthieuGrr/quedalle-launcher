I was tired of launchers that look like airplane cockpits. I wanted one that just launches apps. So I made Quedalle — French slang for "absolutely nothing":

Quedalle in useless widgets.
Quedalle in flashy animations.
Quedalle in endless menus.

But also:

Quedalle in ads.
Quedalle in tracking.
Quedalle in mandatory logins.
Quedalle in paid features.
Quedalle in internet permissions.

Just a launcher. A tile grid. Your apps. That's it.

TILES

- App tiles — with live notification badge counts
- Spacer tiles — colored blanks to structure your grid
- Divider tiles — thin lines between tile groups

CUSTOMIZATION

- Drag and drop to reorder any tile
- 12 color presets per tile, plus app-color and transparent
- Inline app search

PRIVACY

- No internet permission
- No analytics, cloud sync, or accounts
- Open source under GPL-3.0
